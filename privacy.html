Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Sacred Texts is a multi-faith scripture reader published by BannedProduct Media Inc. Your privacy is simple to describe because the app is built to need nothing from you.

What we collect

Nothing. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

Data stored on your device

Your notes, highlights, bookmarks, reading position, and display settings are saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else. You can export it to a file you control, or clear it any time from Settings → Clear all data. Uninstalling the app removes it.

Network use

All scripture texts are bundled inside the app and read offline. The app does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests to our servers (we operate none for this app).

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

Questions: open an issue on the project repository, or contact BannedProduct Media Inc.
